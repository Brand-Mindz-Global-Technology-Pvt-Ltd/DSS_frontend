What is DSS Tenkasi?

DSS Tenkasi is a hyperlocal platform that connects people in Tenkasi with nearby services, shops, and businesses. It helps users quickly find what they need within their local area.

Who can use this platform?

Anyone in Tenkasi can use DSS—customers looking for services, and local businesses who want to reach nearby customers.

What kind of services are available?

You can find:
  • Local shops
  • Home services
  • Food & restaurants
  • Freelancers & professionals
  • Emergency services

All within Tenkasi.

How does DSS Tenkasi help local businesses?

It helps businesses:
  • Get more local customers
  • Increase visibility
  • Receive direct enquiries
  • Build trust within the community

Is it free to use?

Yes, users can browse and connect with local services for free. Businesses may have optional plans for better visibility.
